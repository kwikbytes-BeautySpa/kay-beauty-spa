Benefits

Post Op Care Package :

A comprehensive suite of post-operative services tailored for recovery and body contouring. Includes gentle care for recent post-op patients (with or without drains), full-body care, and ongoing maintenance to promote healing, comfort, and skin health. Incorporates therapeutic massage and taping to reduce swelling, enhance body contouring, and sustain post-operative results.

Butt Lift and Enhance :

A non-invasive treatment to lift, tone, and enhance the buttocks, using specialized techniques to sculpt the area while reducing cellulite and improving skin texture for a smoother, more defined look.

Sauna Wrap :

A full-body heated wrap treatment to promote sweating, detoxification, and skin rejuvenation, with specialized post-operative applications to enhance recovery and maintain results.

Facial Treatments Package :

A comprehensive range of facial treatments tailored to diverse skin needs. Includes specialized facials like the Acne Facial to target acne-prone skin with cleansing and calming techniques, the Extraction Facial for deep cleansing with manual blackhead removal, and the Teen Facial designed for young skin to address oiliness and acne. The Kay Signature Facial offers a customized luxury experience, while Men's Cleansing and Deep Cleansing Facials focus on refreshing and intensive care for male skin. The Back Facial treats acne and oiliness on the back, and the Head Spa with Mini Facial combines scalp rejuvenation with facial care. Advanced exfoliation options like Chemical Peel, Hydro Dermabrasion, and Micro Dermabrasion improve skin texture, tone, and hydration for a radiant complexion.

Waxing Services Package :

A complete range of waxing treatments designed for smooth, hair-free skin across various body areas. Includes Brazilian (V) [Bikini Line, Butt Strip] and Bikini Full (V) for thorough hair removal in the bikini area, alongside Full Arm [Hands and Fingers] and Half Arm [Upper Arm, Elbow] for polished arm skin. Full Leg [Not Bikini Line], Lower Leg [Knees, Feet, Toes], and Upper Leg [Knees, Inner Thighs, Not Bikini Line] provide comprehensive leg waxing options. Facial waxing covers Chin, Upper Lip [Not Lower Lip], and Upper Lip [Including Lower Lip] for precise hair removal, while Full Back and Under Arm [Including Lower Arm or Under Arm only] target larger areas. Nose [Inside Nostrils] waxing ensures a clean, refined look.
